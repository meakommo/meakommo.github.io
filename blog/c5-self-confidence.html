Self confindence

  • What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

    • Mindfulness is the lens that we observe the world and ourselves. It is not a weapon for defense or to attack what we see through the lens but instead it is a tool to clearly observe what is there. It does not refer to the past or the future it only looks at the now.

    Awareness is the realization of mindfulness. we do not retain the or discard the information that we discover we only maintain the awareness that it exists.

  • Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

    • The body scan technique allowed me to consider why I might be in pain or to narrow down where I am actually hurting. It also allowed me to consider parts of myself that I often neglect and address why that might be.
