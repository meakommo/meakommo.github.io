  • What 'scope' is, and how does it works in JavaScript?

    • Variable Scope is essentially where you have a box and that box has some stuff in it, scope defines who is allowed to look inside of said box . scope is declared based on where the box is, so lets say that we have a town, if the box is in the street then everyone is allowed to look at what is inside but if the box is inside the house then only the people inside of the house are allowed to look inside of the box. This is essentially how variable scope works. If a box is at the top of the program then it is global and everything can use it but if it is inside of a function than only the function can look at the variable.
