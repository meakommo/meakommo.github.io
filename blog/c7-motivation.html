  • How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

    • Pleasure is the Rockstar outlook on life, it is the instant gratification you get from winning the lottery or getting a bonus at work. However this way of life is not something that everyone can constantly enjoy unless they are a rockstar or millionaire. For the more down to earth and normal people we only get to enjoy lifes pleasures in small doses but we can structure our lives so that we can enjoy lifes pleasures more often by doing a job that we enjoy , by engaging in hobbies and surrounding ourselves with people who we enjoy the company of.

    Passion or as Search inside yourself describes it "Flow" is the point at which we enjoy doing something so much our work ethic is optimal. By doing a job that we enjoy we find that we never really work we are always playing and this causes us to be passionate about what we are doing and in turn causes us to put in more effort.

    Purpose Is the feeling that we are working toward something that is greater than ourselves that we truly care about. and this in turn causes us to reach our peak performance level allowing us to exceed our regular levels of efficiency.
